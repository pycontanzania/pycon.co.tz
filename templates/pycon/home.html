PYCON TANZANIA.

Join us for the 5th Python Conference at the University of Dar es Salaam, Mlimani City Campus.. where Python developers, enthusiasts and learners from across East Africa will gather to share knowledge, build connections and celebrate out growing tech community.

Our Programme

What to expect at the 5th Annual Python Conference 2024

Workshops

Workshops are interactive sessions where you can learn new skills, get hands-on experience with Python, and even build something together. These sessions are designed to be interactive, engaging, and fun for everyone.

Talks

Talks are 30 minute long technical presentations, on any topic that might interest the Python community. It doesn't have to be something you've created, feel free to share something you've learned or a technique you think others will find useful.

Main Conference

PyCon Tanzania is the premier gathering for Python enthusiasts, developers, and tech innovators from across East Africa and beyond. Join us for an inspiring celebration of Python, featuring engaging talks, hands-on workshops, and unparalleled networking opportunities.

Keynote Speakers

Join us in welcoming our distinguished keynote speakers who will share their insights,
experiences, and vision for Python and technology in Tanzania and beyond. These industry leaders and innovators
will inspire and enlighten attendees with their expertise and passion for Python.
